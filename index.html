Alternate Solutionz is a software development company with a goal to provide the best products to business owners for managing their operations efficiently. We are a team of technology enthusiasts who implement technology according to client needs in order to resolve real business problems.

We specialize in purpose-built business software — from inventory and accounting to payroll, attendance, school systems, restaurant & POS solutions, gym management, and production management. Based in Karachi, our mission is to provide complete IT solutions on a single platform, making business management simpler, faster, and more accurate.
